# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing for mistakes
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, insulting or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others’ private information without permission
- Discrimination or exclusionary behavior
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

Maintainers may take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate, including warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban from the project.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories
- Issues and pull requests
- Discussions and chats
- Social media related to the project

It also applies when an individual represents the project in public spaces.

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the maintainers at:

**admin@thefuselabs.com**

All reports will be handled confidentially and reviewed promptly.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers will follow these general steps:

1. **Warning** – A private warning explaining the violation
2. **Temporary Ban** – Temporary restriction from participation
3. **Permanent Ban** – Removal from the community
